Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is DEVCONF 2026?

DEVCONF 2026 will take place in July 2026. It is a completely virtual conference accessible worldwide.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes, all registered participants will get lifetime access to the recorded sessions within 24 hours of the live event.

Can I get a refund if I cannot attend?

We offer a full refund up to 7 days before the event starts. Please contact our support team for assistance.

Is there any certificate provided?

Yes, all registered attendees who complete the core tracks will receive an official verifiable digital certificate.

Can I ask questions to the speakers during live sessions?

Absolutely! Every session includes a dedicated live Q&A segment where you can interact directly via chat or audio.

Do you offer any student discounts?

Yes, we have limited student scholarships. Please submit your academic ID through our registration portal to apply.
